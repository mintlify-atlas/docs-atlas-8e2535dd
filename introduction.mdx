## What is Vue QR Style?

Vue QR Style is a premium Vue 3 component that makes it easy to generate beautiful, highly customizable QR codes in your Vue applications. Built with the Composition API and TypeScript, it provides a simple yet powerful interface for creating QR codes with advanced styling options.

Powered by [qr-code-styling](https://github.com/kozakdenys/qr-code-styling), Vue QR Style wraps this robust library into a reactive Vue component that updates in real-time as props change, giving you instant visual feedback as you customize your QR codes.

## Use Cases

Vue QR Style is perfect for a variety of applications:

- **Marketing Materials** - Generate branded QR codes for print and digital campaigns
- **Product Packaging** - Create QR codes that link to product information or authentication
- **Event Tickets** - Generate unique QR codes for event check-ins
- **Business Cards** - Add personalized QR codes to digital or physical business cards
- **Payment Systems** - Create QR codes for payment links or cryptocurrency wallets
- **Authentication** - Generate QR codes for two-factor authentication flows
- **App Deep Links** - Create QR codes that open specific screens in your mobile app
